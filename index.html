Hi Fellow Community Members and Friends,

We have reached out to community organizations where One Bay and our Schools have a strong relationship to see how we might support them with the vulnerable members of our greater community that they support in this time of need. If you are able to provide any assistance it would be greatly appreciated.

This is a “wish list” of the organizations that have responded to date. For safety and simplicity's sake, please either ship the items you are able to donate directly to the organization or drop them off during the noted hours. Each page has a summary of what each group is doing to help those in our community. Thank you for considering.

Sincerely,
Students, parents, and alumni of Bay Area schools
