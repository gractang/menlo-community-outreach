Salvation Army

The Salvation Army provides shelter for working homeless men and overnight stay for the homeless along with lunch and dinner year-round, as well as provide hearty weekday lunch for Seniors and food bags for families year-round. The Family Services provides several services to assist with housing, homeless, families and Seniors. The hygiene kits would be distributed at either our Family Services area or at Emmanuel House (the homeless shelter).

Organizational contact: Liwayway Gimenez, liwayway.gimenez@usw.salvationarmy.org
